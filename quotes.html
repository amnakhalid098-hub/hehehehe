words you can come back to

read slowly. or don’t. they’ll wait.

Some people feel like home, even when everything else feels unfamiliar.
You are allowed to take up space gently.
Nothing is wrong with you for needing softness.
Even slow days are still days lived.
You don’t owe the world your constant strength.
There are parts of you that are healing even when you don’t notice.
You’re doing better than your doubts let you believe.
Rest is allowed here.
You don’t have to explain your tiredness.
