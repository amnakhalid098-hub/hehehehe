If today felt like too much, you don’t have to explain it to
anyone. Some days feel heavy without a clear reason, no
big disaster, no obvious mistake, just a quiet weight sitting
on your chest. And that doesn’t mean you’re weak or ungrateful
or doing life wrong. It just means you’re human.

You don’t need to be productive here. You don’t need
to “get over it.” Rest isn’t something you earn after surviving
the day, it’s something you’re allowed to take during it.

Stay here for a moment. Breathe a little slower. Let your shoulders
drop.Nothing is asking anything from you right now, and nothing
needs fixing immediately. Even heavy days pass — and you don’t
have to carry them alone.
