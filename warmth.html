Comfort doesn’t always arrive as answers or advice.
Sometimes it’s just a quiet presence, the feeling that thought
of you gently, without expecting anything in return.

You don’t have to be strong here. You don’t have to
explain why you’re tired, or why you feel the way you do. You’re
allowed to be soft. You’re allowed to be unsure.

Think of this moment like sitting near a window on a calm evening,
nothing urgent, nothing loud. Just warmth, slowly reaching you. If the
world felt a little cold today, let this be the place where it doesn’t.
