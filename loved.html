you are loved

You matter in ways you don’t even notice. In
the way you show up quietly. In the small kindnesses
you don’t think count. In how your presence makes
things feel softer without you trying.

You don’t have to be extraordinary every day to be
meaningful. You don’t have to prove your worth through
effort, success, or perfection.

Even on the days you doubt yourself, even when you feel
replaceable or invisible, you are still leaving an imprint. You
are still important. You are still enough.

The version of you that exists right now, exactly as you are,
deserves patience and love. Especially from yourself.
